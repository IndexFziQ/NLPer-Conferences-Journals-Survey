## 📊 Quick Stats & Tips

### 📈 **Impact Factor Trends 2024**
- **Highest IF**: Nature Machine Intelligence (25.9)
- **Rising Stars**: TACL (10.9), TAC (13.99)
- **Stable Performers**: CL (6.2), JMLR (6.0)

### 💡 **Submission Tips**
1. **🎯 ARR Strategy**: Many conferences now use ACL Rolling Review - submit early!
2. **📝 Quality over Quantity**: Focus on fewer, high-quality submissions
3. **🔄 Rebuttal Preparation**: Prepare for reviewer feedback rounds
4. **📅 Plan Ahead**: Major conferences have 6-12 month review cycles

### 🌟 **Hot Topics 2025**
- 🤖 Large Language Models (LLMs)
- 🧠 Multimodal AI
- 🛡️ AI Safety & Ethics
- 🌍 Multilingual NLP
- 📊 Evaluation & Benchmarking
